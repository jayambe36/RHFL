AN APPEAL TO DONORS TO HELP “BABY.KUMARI AADYA ASHOK” WHO IS SUFFERING FROM “RELAPSED ACUTE LYMPHOBLASTIC LEUKEMIA”.

Aadya ashok is just 6-year girl and life has been very hard with her. This 6-year girl has been diagnosed with “RELAPSED ACUTE LYMPHOBLASTIC LEUKEMIA” (Type of blood cancer). In India out of every 10-cancer patient 6 diagnosed with blood cancer and Aadya is one of them. She has to undergo “Chemotherapy and other supporting treatment”.
Currently Aadya is under treatment at Mazumdar Shaw Medical Center,Bangalore. And the treatment will prolong for more than 3 Years. This little child who has not even cherished her life has to fight for it.
Aadya’s Father is working as Farmer and mother is house wife, live with their joint family. Mr. Ashok is the only earning member in the family of 3 people and has no support from anywhere as they are totally depending on him.
The world has been cruelled on Mr. Ashok by putting him in this situation to see his little girl like this and not being able to bear his Daughter’s treatment expenses.

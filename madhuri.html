EMERGENCY MEDICAL SUPPORT

  1. Donate Now

Rajkumar and Madhuri were at the peak of their joy when they learnt that Madhuri was pregnant. But soon their dream turned into their worst nightmare. Madhuri’s baby was born prematurely in her seventh month of pregnancy. Due to this, she suffers from CPAP support, OG feeds a DDL-4 frequent desaturations and feed intolerance. It has been fifteen days since the baby has been admitted to the Neo-natal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) care of the Renova Neelima Hospital, Sanatnagar, Hyderabad.
The doctors have proposed a month-long NICU care for her. The treatment would cost around Rs 6,00,000/- (Rupees Six Lakhs Only). Madhuri's husband, Mr Rajkumar is a lecturer in a local college and earns a meagre salary. They have already mortgaged their only house and are living in a rented apartment in Sanatnagar, Hyderabad. You are the only hope they have, so please help them in their difficult times.
“My dream turned into my worst nightmare. I don’t know what to do or where to look for help. You are our only hope, so I plead with you to please donate as much as possible. I want to see my daughter all grown up, well educated, financially independent and you are the only hope me and Madhuri have for our baby girl.” – Rajkumar (Baby’s father)

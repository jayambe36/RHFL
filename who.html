Real Happiness Of Life Foundation,We work in the best interest of Needy.
We focus on the basics such as Education, Health, Food and Shelter. Our approach is holistic and our style hands on. We are on the ground, where it matters most for the child. We enable both families and communities. We have several grass roots initiatives.
We will be completing our first decade of growth soon. We started working in 2021. We are building on our many successes and reaching out to more and more marginalized People

VISION

MISSION
Real Happiness Of Life Foundation for social changed and inclusion works for the social development and integration of under privileged individuals groups and communities in India. Eradicating all People of sorrow, prevailing truth, love and peace, practicing oneness, and celebrating humanity.
